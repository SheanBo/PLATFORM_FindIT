## 💬 Key Questions Answered

**Q: Is the app "broken" or just needs polish?**  
A: The app works well functionally! It's 95% functional, 60% visual polish. This audit addresses the 40% polish gap.

**Q: Can we do mobile and accessibility in parallel?**  
A: Yes! They affect different parts of the codebase, so 2 engineers can work on them simultaneously.

**Q: What's the ROI on these improvements?**  
A: Expected improvements:
- Mobile engagement: +40-50%
- User satisfaction: +35%
- Task completion time: -30%
- Support costs: -20%
- Training time: -40%

**Q: Why is color inconsistency a critical issue?**  
A: 70% of pages still use gray colors instead of the new navy/gold theme. Creates impression of unfinished work and undermines the redesign investment.

**Q: Can we skip Phase 1 and jump to features (Phase 4)?**  
A: Not recommended. Phase 1 establishes visual consistency and fixes accessibility gaps (legal requirement). Takes only 3-4 days but has massive impact.

**Q: What about the design system?**  
A: The foundation is solid (7/10). After implementing Phases 1-3, it will be stronger. Phase 5 documents it for long-term maintainability.
